What is Short-Term Insurance?

Short-term insurance provides financial protection for your belongings, vehicles, home, and other assets against unexpected events such as accidents, theft, fire, or natural disasters. Unlike long-term policies, these plans are flexible and can be adjusted to meet your immediate needs.
