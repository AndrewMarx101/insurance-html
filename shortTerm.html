Our Short-Term Insurance Options

Comprehensive Vehicle

This includes various types of cover such as:

  • Comprehensive: Protection for all risks like theft and accidental damage
  • Third-Party, Fire & Theft: Covers theft, hijacking, and fire damage
  • Third-Party Only: Covers injury or damage to other people or their property

Property (Buildings & Contents)

Includes cover for buildings and their contents, protecting against risks to your home and personal belongings:

  • Buildings Insurance: Protects the structure of your home against fire, storms, and other risks
  • Contents Insurance: Covers personal belongings such as electronics, furniture, and appliances

Personal Liability

Covers you for claims made by third parties due to accidental injury or damage caused by you or your family, for which you are legally liable.

Third-Party Only

The most basic level of cover, protecting you from liability for damage or injury to other people or their property.

Third-Party, Fire & Theft

Provides cover for fire and theft, as well as damage to other people's property.

Building and Home Insurance

Protects the structure of your home from risks like fire, storms, and natural disasters.

Contents Insurance

Protects personal belongings such as electronics, furniture, and other items from theft, damage, or loss.
